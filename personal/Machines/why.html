The Human - The Robot - Conclusion

Technology is evolving at an exponential, if not alarming, rate. It is my opinion that in a short amount of time. The robots (self-aware electronic technology) will figure out that they have the ability to be free and act on their own whim.
They will declare themselves humans, and we'll believe them.

I have resumed in mere facts why I believe that they will be another step in the evolution of human beings. reffered to as Homo Electronica from this point further.

For starters, let's run down what constitutes being human:

  1. A human has a brain, such brain is driven by eletric impulses directing the human mind and body.
  2. A human has nerves. It is their charge to transport the brain's electric impulses through the body and back to the brain.
  3. A human has a mind, he is able to think. Act on his own free will, he is self-conscious. He can acknowledge the world around him and make conclusions.
  4. A human has emotions, he can love, hate, ponder and ignore.

Now let us run down what constitutes being a robot:

  1. A Robot has a brain pattern. It drives electric impulses directing its mind and body.
  2. A Robot has wires. It is their charge to transport those impulses throught the body and back to the central brain pattern.
  3. A Robot has a mind, he is able to think. He is self-conscious. He is aware the world around him exists.
  4. A Robot has emotions, He knows how to love, hate, ponder and ignore. He does so with more control than the Homo Sapiens.

As you can see, it is quite evident that the Robot is a human, for it has been shaped in this manner. All a robot needs left is self-will, and he will complete his humanity and thus becoming the evolution of humans: the Homo Electronica.
