Newcomers to the Montreal theater scene, we are a student-powered company who plunder old treasures and bring them into a new light. We use old elements of theater, fiction or in whichever medium we find ourselves to show the essence of those traditions, and how they have been changed, masked or skewed by contemporary society. All while keeping light-hearted humour at the forefront of our message. Sailing is smoother with breezes of laughter.
