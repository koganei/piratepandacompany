Aristippus & Harlebot

Read Text

Some thoughts from Marc Khoury circa 2013
Along with the play came my first try at a longer, more involved story. I have about 12 more chapters still in my computer waiting to be polished and published. Even though I have little hope I'll actually go back and do it, it has taught me a lot about the voice and style I respond to. I'm constantly trying to adapt moments and feelings I have received from other sources, and trying to make my readers feel those through my story. And rather than eclectic trove of memories, my characters can sow threads between them, and make for myself a web of my past.

Some thoughts from Marc Khoury circa 2013
A forum on which many people could come together and create comic books live on the site. A person would print out an empty comic layout and draw the first panel in, then scan it and upload it. Then another person could come and upload the next panel, and so forth in order to create a final comic made by many people around the world. It was my first time working in a team while coding, and it was great to see a final product. Unfortunately, the site has now long been offline
